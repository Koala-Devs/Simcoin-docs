Complete command reference for SimCoin — the Discord economy bot set in Simora City. 100+ commands across 13 categories.

SimCoin is currently in Beta. Commands may change as we tune the economy. Join the Discord server for updates.

What is SimCoin?

SimCoin is a Discord economy bot set in Simora City — a sleek, ambition-driven city where everyone is chasing wealth, status and power. Start with nothing in The Slums and fight your way to the Financial District.

FeatureDetails
/startHow you begin — creates your Simora City profile
CurrencySC — Simora Coin. Wallet (spendable, robbable) + Bank (safe)
Districts6 areas to unlock — each with unique jobs, risks and rewards
Reputation8 ranks earned through jobs and challenges — gates district access
Commands100+ slash commands all in one Bot
Works inDMs (personal commands) + Servers (social commands)
