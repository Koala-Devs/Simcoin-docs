Commands · Crime

CRIME

25+ crimes across 5 categories. District-locked, risky, and reputation-costing — even when you succeed.

🚔
Crime costs reputation whether you succeed or fail. Getting caught means jail time + fines. Use /crime lawyer for 50% sentence reduction.
CategoryCrimesNotes
StreetPickpocket, Shoplifting, Purse Snatch, Car TheftLow risk, low reward
ViolentMugging, Robbery, Home Invasion, Armed RobberyHigher reward, higher jail risk
White CollarFraud, Embezzlement, Insider TradingFinancial District only
CyberATM Skimming, Bank Hack, Data Breach, RansomwareUnderground access required

/crime [action] [target]Commit a crime or check jail status
SERVER▼

How it works

  • action: any of the 25+ crime types
  • target: optional @user for crimes targeting players
  • District-locked — some crimes only in specific areas
  • Success rate: crime type + equipped gear bonuses
  • Success: receive reward (fixed SC or % of target's wallet)
  • Failure: fine + possible jail sentence + rep loss
⚖️
Use /crime jail_status to check time remaining and bail cost. Use /crime lawyer for 50% sentence reduction (costs SC).
